The Phillipines

Located in Southeast Asia, the Philippines is an archipelagic country composed of over 7,000 islands. Boasting a remarkable heritage, this country has a vibrant history that has shaped its character. With a populace surpassing 100 million, the Philippines embraces an incredibly diverse culture, which is a fusion of its indigenous origins, the impact of Spanish colonization, and its connections with numerous other nations From bustling metropolitan cities to remote tropical islands, the country offers a wide range of living experiences.



Quick Facts

• Location: Southeast Asia, Western Pacific Ocean.

• Capital: Manila.

• Population: Over 113 million.

• Official Languages: Filipino and English.
